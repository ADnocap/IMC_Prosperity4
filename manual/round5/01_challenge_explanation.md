## Rules

- **Budget:** 1,000,000 XIRECs
- **Fee formula:**
  ```
  fee = (volume_for_specific_product / 100) * (volume_for_specific_product / 100)
  ```
  _(applied per product against budget)_
- You are allowed to distribute **less than** 100% of the budget.
- You are **not allowed** to distribute more than 100% of the budget.
- Used budget will be subtracted from your trade PnL.
- Unused budget will **not** be added to your profit — it simply expires worthless.

## Grading

- A return range and anchor are defined for every product (e.g., 1-day returns).
- How people submit their answers will move the actual returns within the range — if everybody thinks something is a strong buy, the price will go up more than the anchor (within the range).
- It's up to you to critically read text and see that some news is good / bad / already priced in, etc.
